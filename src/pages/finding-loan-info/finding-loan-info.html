finding my loan info

Basically. . .

There are two major types of loans federal loans that you get by completeing FAFSA and private loans that you get from a bank. If you have private loans, call your bank or go to the loan office and ask for the information regarding your loans. If you are dealing with federal loans there are a couple of things you will have to do.

Federal loan information checklist

Create an account at StudentLoans.gov

This will require:

  • Your social security number
  • FAFSA ID & password
 Figure out who your loan servicer is Create an account with your loan servicer
