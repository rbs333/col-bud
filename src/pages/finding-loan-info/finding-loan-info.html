finding my loan info

Typical loan amounts

  • Average student loans for undergrad: $30,000
  • Federal student loan rate is roughly 5%
  • Private loan rates depend on a number of factors and can be higher or lower than federal rates

Finding loan info

Federal loans

  1. Create an account at StudentLoans.gov
    This will require:
    • Your social security number
    • FAFSA ID & password
  2. Figure out who your loan servicer is once you have logged in.
  3. Create an account with your loan servicer.

Private loans

Contact your bank that provided you the loan and ask for that information.
